GAME OF THRONES TOUR


Duration: Approximately 2.5 hours
Location: Dubrovnik Old Town
Accessibility: Wheelchair accessible (except for St. Lawrence Fortress)
Notes: It is highly recommended to bring comfortable shoes, a bottle of water, and casual dress

Welcome to King’s Landing dear travelers. The city is made brighter by your presence… Come with me on this tour to follow the footsteps of your favorite characters and discover the famous filming locations.

Since Season 2, Dubrovnik has been a main filming location for King’s Landing, the fictional capital of the Seven Kingdoms in the TV show Game of Thrones. During this tour, accompanied by photos, we will follow the footsteps of your favorite characters and discover famous filming locations, such as:
- Red Keep
- Blackwater Bay
- Littlefinger’s brothel
- Cersei’s walk of shame
- Joffrey’s and Margaery’s wedding
- House of the Undying

The end of the tour will be at the official GOT shop where you will have opportunity to grab a souvenir and take a picture on the Iron Throne.

Apart from showing all the Game of Thrones highlights, I will also provide some history of the Old town and the region.
